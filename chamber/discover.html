New Park Announced Yesterday

There is a new park. The local area of Pitkin and Ute will have a new park location for the families in the Downtown area. The homeless have been using this area to camp and sleep. It will be renevated to host family events in the near future.

Weather

Fall is Coming

There are a few things that may need attention now before old man Winter arrives. You can find many deals throughout town on some of these services. The cold comes on fast so make sure all your heating needs are ready to go before it starts. Is your car ready for the winter? Tires and heat are a thing to consider during this time as well.

Discover the Local Fruits and Vegtables

Apples from Z's Orchard

Come and visit the local orchard. Hot cider is available to all customers for free. Z's Orchard is a local orchard that has been in business for over 50 years. They have a variety of apples and peaches. They also have a variety of other fruits and vegetables. They are located in Palisade, Colorado. They are open from 9am to 5pm Monday through Saturday. They are closed on Sundays.
