Site Purpose

This is the town I grew up in over the summer with my father.

The purpose of this site is to provide information about the Grand Junction area. The Chamber of Commerce, its members, and the community are all important parts of what makes this community one of a kind.

Scenerio 3

Color Schema

The color schema for this site will be shades of blue and white. The area is a red rock area with lovely blue skys.

The color for the background will be a cream color. #fbf3e0

The text color is a dark red. It does pass the contrast ratio. #3c1616

The extra contrast colors will be to pop some color in the site. #f5d58a

The other extra color will be a dark blue for some needed contrast. #02041c

Typography

The fonts used for this site will be Libre Baskerville - Headlines.

The fonts for text will be Source Sans Regular and italic.
